Our after school organization is geared towards helping young minds to develop their business leadership, communication skills and strong self confidence by inspiring young people to be science and technology leaders in the near future. As part of the district wide STEM Initiative, Swampscott High School began the Robotics Team during the 2011-2012 school year.

The SHS Robotics Team annually participates in an international program run by a nonforprofit known as FIRST (For the Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology). Our team, #4311, is one of thousands that compete in their competition.

FIRST allows students to work alongside mentors from the engineering and science fields while learning to use industry standard tools, equipment and software. Over $15 million dollars in scholarships are available annually to students involved in FIRST. 

Competing in robotics competitions requires funding. Our annual team budget is $10,000. These funds will allow our team the chance to compete in district events and qualify for the Boston Championship event. There is no line item in the Swampscott Public Schools District budget to support this out-of-school time club; therefore we need to seek sponsorships.

The Swampscott Robotics Team is seeking various levels of sponsorship for the 2019 competition season.  Please contact us at swampscottcurrents@gmail.com if you are interested in supporting/sponsoring us.
