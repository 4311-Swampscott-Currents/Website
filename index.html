Home

Welcome to the home of the Swampscott High School Robotics Team, the 4311 Swampscott Currents. Every year, our group of dedicated members participates in FIRST Robotics Competition, where each enrolled team is given six weeks to build a robot that plays a specified game. While engaging in FRC, students learn not only how to build a robot, but teamwork, financial and social skills from which they can benefit later on in life.
